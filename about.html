about me

Hi!

Who is this panda crazy girl, you ask?

My name's Stacy and I'm a Bay Area native, UCLA graduate, and incoming medical student (I sure do love school). I started sharing my thoughts with the world to document my study abroad experience in London during the fall of 2015 on my WordPress, but realized I had more thoughts to express even after I returned home. During my time off after undergrad, I figured that with all the different parts of my life that I want to share with others, it'd be cool to make my own space for it all using my rusty ol' web design skills...and here you have it! (The panda theme was a no brainer.) The site might make no sense but I'm not here to trend on Instagram or be deemed groundbreaking news - I'm here to share what I'm up to, in a hopefully aesthetically pleasing fashion, for anyone who might find anything here entertaining or helpful.

I'll be mindblown if anyone takes me up on this, but please feel free to email any inquiries to stacyma@ucla.edu. Hope you enjoy! :)
