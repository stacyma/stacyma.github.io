about me

Hi!

Who is this panda crazy girl, you ask?

My name's Stacy and I'm a Bay Area native, UCLA graduate, and incoming medical student. I started sharing my thoughts with the world to document my study abroad experience in London during the fall of 2015 on my WordPress, and continued doing so after I returned home since I so enjoyed the platform. During my time off after undergrad, I figured that with all the different parts of my life that I want to share with others, it'd be cool to make my own space for it all using my rusty (really rusty) ol' web design skills...and here you have it! Posts written before I made this site are directly transferred from my blog. I simply hope to share what I'm up to for anyone who might find anything here entertaining or helpful.

Feel free to email any inquiries to stacyma@ucla.edu. Hope you enjoy! :)
