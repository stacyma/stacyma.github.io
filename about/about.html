about me

Hi!

Who is this panda crazy girl, you ask?

My name's Stacy and I'm a Bay Area native, UCLA graduate, and incoming medical student (oh, how I must love school). I started sharing my thoughts with the world to document my study abroad experience in London during the fall of 2015 on my WordPress, but soon found I had more thoughts to express even after I returned home. During my time off after undergrad, I figured that with all the different parts of my life that I want to share with others, it'd be cool to make my own space for it all using my rusty ol' web design skills...and here you have it! (If you know me at all, you'll get the panda theme.) This site might make no sense but I'm not here to break the news - I'm just sharing what I'm up to, in a hopefully aesthetically pleasing fashion, for anyone who might find anything here entertaining or helpful.

Feel free to email any inquiries to stacyma@ucla.edu (I will be mindblown to receive any). Hope you enjoy! :)
